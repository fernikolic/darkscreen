# Bridge Competitive Analysis

Comparison of 6 bridge apps.

## Key Insights

- **complexity**: Most complex bridge: lifi (35 screens)
- **design-trend**: Color scheme: 6 dark, 5 light out of 6 apps
- **accessibility**: Jargon: 6 beginner-friendly, 4 expert-level out of 6 apps
