# Infrastructure Competitive Analysis

Comparison of 3 infrastructure apps.

## Key Insights

- **complexity**: Most complex infrastructure: lnbits (53 screens)
- **design-trend**: Color scheme: 3 dark, 2 light out of 3 apps
- **accessibility**: Jargon: 3 beginner-friendly, 2 expert-level out of 3 apps
