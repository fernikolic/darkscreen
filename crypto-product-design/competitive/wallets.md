## Key Insights

- **complexity**: Most complex wallet: coinbase-wallet (70 screens)
- **design-trend**: Color scheme: 21 dark, 24 light out of 28 apps
- **accessibility**: Jargon: 23 beginner-friendly, 17 expert-level out of 28 apps
