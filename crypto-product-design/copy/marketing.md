## Jargon Level Distribution

### Dominant (70%+ adoption)

- **intermediate** — 91% (96 apps)
- **beginner** — 81% (85 apps)

### Common (30-70% adoption)

- **none** — 70% (73 apps)
- **expert** — 65% (68 apps)

### Emerging

- beginner-to-intermediate — 9% (9 apps)
- beginner-intermediate — 9% (9 apps)
- intermediate-expert — 5% (5 apps)
- intermediate-to-expert — 3% (3 apps)

- beginner (humanized address format, casual tone) (1 apps)
