# Navigation Patterns

Analysis of navigation across 105 crypto apps (2588 screens).

### Dominant (70%+ adoption)

- **navigation-bar** — 75% (79 apps)

### Common (30-70% adoption)

- **header-navigation** — 42% (44 apps)
- **sidebar-navigation** — 40% (42 apps)
- **Tab Navigation** — 33% (35 apps)
